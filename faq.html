🚘 General & Booking

A VTC (Voiture de Transport avec Chauffeur) is a licensed, pre-booked private hire car service. Unlike a regular Paris taxi you hail on the street, a VTC is reserved in advance with a confirmed, fixed price. Your driver is assigned to your booking, will track your flight in real time, and will be waiting for you at the airport arrivals hall with a name board — ready to help with your luggage and take you directly to your destination.

Booking is simple. Just contact us by phone on +33 (0)1 43 327 081 or email us at Contact@groupbluevanparis.com with your travel details including: flight number, arrival date and time, pickup location, destination address, number of passengers, and any special requirements such as a baby seat. We will confirm availability and send you a fixed price. Once you confirm, your booking is secured.

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability, especially during peak travel periods such as summer, school holidays, and public holidays. However, we also accept last-minute bookings subject to driver availability. For large groups or special VIP requirements, booking several days in advance is advisable.

Absolutely. We encourage you to book your return journey at the same time. Simply provide us with your departure flight details, and we will arrange for a driver to collect you from your hotel, apartment, or other address in Paris and take you to the airport in good time for your flight. Booking both legs together may also attract a discounted combined rate.

All our prices are fixed and agreed upfront before your journey begins. There are no meters, no traffic surcharges, no late-night supplements, and no hidden fees. The price you receive in your booking confirmation is exactly the price you pay — nothing more. This is one of the key advantages of booking a VTC service over a standard Paris taxi.

We accept payment in cash (Euros) and by major credit or debit card. Payment is typically collected at the end of your journey or as agreed at the time of booking. If you require a receipt for business expenses, we are happy to provide one.

We understand that travel plans can change. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify your booking. Cancellations made with reasonable advance notice are handled without charge. For specific terms relating to your booking, please ask at the time of reservation and we will explain our policy clearly.

📞 Flight Delays & Changes

We track all incoming flights in real time using your flight number. If your flight is delayed — even by several hours — your driver automatically adjusts their arrival time accordingly. You do not need to call us. There is no extra charge for waiting due to a flight delay. Your driver will still be there, name board in hand, when you come through arrivals.

If your flight is cancelled, please contact us as soon as possible by phone on +33 (0)1 43 327 081. We will rearrange your booking to match your new travel plans at no additional administrative charge. We understand that flight cancellations are outside your control and will always do our best to accommodate any changes to your itinerary.
